Everything but cars

Alternatives to cars

  • The bus...
    You might think that buses are worse than cars because they're so big, always stuck in traffic too, and pollute more than cars. But studies have shown that this isn't really true. Yes, buses pollute more than cars, but a car carrying one person emits 40kg of CO2 for 160 kilometers driven while a full bus only emits 6kg of CO2.
  • Trams are even better than buses...
    Yes, they don't go as far as we'd all like them to. But the network is expanding with 2 and a half new tramlines coming before the end of 2026 in Lyon and its surroundings. They can't be stuck in traffic as they have their own lane. In addition to this, they don't have the 30 km/h speed limit that cars have in Lyon! So many pros and almost no cons!
  • The metro
    Metros are awesome when combinated with trams! They transport you to the places where all tram lines and bus lines connect. It's awesome
  • TCL's park-and-ride service...
    Living far from Lyon and coming every day can be a pain. Why don't you drive to the nearest TCL parking lot, leave your car there, and ride the bus, the tram or even the metro in some cases? It's free as long as you validate a ticket or your TCL card.They open early, close late. With 21 parl-and-ride places, about 7000 parking spaces, you now have no excuses left not to use your car. There are also park-and-ride places for bikes!

LA DOUA CAR-FREE DAY

Thursday, October 6th 2022 is a car-free day at the IUT!

Why is the parking lot closed?

We, a student organization, decided to plan this event to show people that there are alternatives to cars! We aren't trying to tell you that cars are 100% bad, and that you should never go to work or school by car. We simply want people to be aware that there are other solutions to go to the IUT.

Learn more

When will it be closed? How long?

We decided, in accordance with the administration of the IUT, to close it during the entire day of Thursday, October 6th 2022. Before and after this day, the parking lot will be open as it usually is.

Learn more

How can I learn new ways to go to the IUT?

You can either stay on this website and keep on navigating or you can follow us on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram to have day-to-day news on La Doua Car-Free Day. In both ways, be reassured, there is always a way to go to the IUT without you car!
